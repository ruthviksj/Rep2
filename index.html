04 — The Solution · me, briefly

It's not magic. It's three habits.

01 — Diagnose

Start at the slow step.

Before the spec, find the actual constraint. The spec follows the constraint, not the other way around.

02 — Design

Build the system, not just the feature.

A tool is a tool. A system that's measurably faster or cheaper is what stays.

03 — Measure

Instrument before launch.

If we can't see the metric move on a dashboard, we didn't change anything — we just shipped.
