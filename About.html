Slide 03 / 08

03 — Operating principles

Three habits that have stuck.

01

Start at the slow step.

Before the spec, find the actual constraint. The spec follows the constraint, not the other way around.

02

Build the system, not just the feature.

A loan-review tool is a tool. A system that's measurably faster and cheaper is what tends to stick.

03

Instrument before launch.

If we can't see the metric move on a dashboard, I'm not sure if we changed anything. The measurement goes in first.
